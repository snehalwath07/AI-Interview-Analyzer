Step 1: Open the Website
Open the AI Interview Analyzer using the following link:
**https://your-vercel-link.vercel.app/

You will be redirected to the landing page of the application.

---

## Step 2: Explore the Landing Page

The landing page contains:

* Navigation Bar
* Hero Section
* Get Started Free Button
* Start Free Interview Button
* Features Section
* Trusted Companies Section

Read the information to understand the purpose of the platform.

---

## Step 3: Login

Click **"Get Started Free"** or **"Start Free Interview"**.

You will be redirected to the Login page.

Enter your email and password, then click **"Login"**.

After successful login, you will be redirected to the Dashboard.

---

## Step 4: Explore the Dashboard

The dashboard contains:

* AI Interview Performance Overview
* Performance Metrics
* AI Feedback Panel
* Interview Progress
* Sidebar Navigation
* Quick Access to All Features

Users can navigate to different sections using the sidebar.

---

## Step 5: Start AI Interview

Click **"AI Questions"** from the sidebar.

The system will:

* Generate interview questions
* Display one question at a time
* Allow users to answer verbally
* Analyze the response

During the interview the application records:

* Speech
* Confidence
* Eye Contact
* Facial Expressions
* Head Movement
* Speaking Speed

---

## Step 6: View AI Performance Analysis

After answering interview questions, the dashboard displays:

### Confidence Score

Measures the confidence level while speaking.

### Eye Contact Score

Analyzes whether the user maintains proper eye contact.

### Speech Analysis

Evaluates speaking speed and communication quality.

### AI Feedback

Provides personalized suggestions for improving interview performance.

### Interview Progress

Displays the number of completed interview questions.

---

## Step 7: Resume Match Analysis

Click **"Resume Match"** from the sidebar.

Users can upload resumes in the following formats:

* PDF
* DOCX

Paste the Job Description into the provided text area.

Click **"Analyze Match"**.

The system will:

* Read the resume
* Extract text
* Compare resume skills with the job description
* Calculate Resume Match Score
* Detect Missing Skills
* Recommend Matching Skills

---

## Step 8: Review Resume Results

After analysis, the system displays:

### Resume Match Score

Shows how well the uploaded resume matches the job description.

### Missing Skills

Displays important skills found in the job description but missing from the resume.

### Recommended Skills

Shows matching technical skills detected in the resume.

### Resume Improvement Suggestion

Provides guidance to improve the resume and increase the match percentage.

---

## Step 9: Interview History

Click **"History"** from the sidebar.

The History page displays:

* Previous Interview Sessions
* Interview Score
* Date of Attempt

Users can review previous interview performance.

Users can also click **"Delete History"** to remove all stored interview records.

---

## Step 10: Logout

Click the **"Logout"** button from the sidebar.

The application clears the current session and redirects the user back to the Landing Page.

---

#How It Works

### Step 1

User logs into the application.

### Step 2

AI generates interview questions.

### Step 3

The system records speech and facial activity.

### Step 4

AI analyzes interview performance.

### Step 5

Performance metrics and AI feedback are displayed.

### Step 6

Users can upload resumes for Resume Match Analysis.

### Step 7

The system compares resume skills with the Job Description and generates recommendations.
